Educare

Reducing gap between teacher and parent.

march 2, 2019 Completed

About project :

The duration of the project was ten hours. The given assignment is to work on the communication between the teacher and parent for the welfare of their children. After a comprehensive study of the whole system and considering the possible intervention points I came up with the concept of “educare”.

The Brief:

Considering the increasing number of working parents, design an application that allows for sharing of homework, feedback, and other inputs between teachers and parents on a day-to-day basis.

The process flow:

First I started thinking with remembering my kindergarten school days. Actually i am from Andhra Pradesh but my daddy got a government job in west Bengal so we had to move. That time my mother didn't know Bengali which is a regional language there, even thought my mom is a homemaker she didn't know Bengali so that she could communicate with the teachers and my dad was busy with his job.
So basing on this situation I have designed the app Which first includes making the communication experience simple like if the parent don't know English then the parent can convert the message into regional language my pressing on the text.
I have made designs aptly which directly resembles the purpose of the app.I first thought of making a chatting sort of interface but then got a conclusion that there are a lot many Messenger available to chat but this is completely changing the purpose of the app.
So I designed the app in such a way that the parents and teacher can push some notifications basing on the purpose they the using the app. The App basically includes four tasks-
1) Home work
2) Feedback
3) Seeking permission
4) Invitation According the school curriculum these are the basic functionalities between parents and teacher about the child.



"According to Kementrian Pendidikan dan Kebudayaan Republik Indonesia: The key to the success of education is not only from the curriculum and infrastructure that is adequate but also from educators such as teachers and parents."



Goals:

Parents of students already have an awareness of the importance of attention to children’s development in school, but do not know how to do it easily
Even if doing so, parents must contact the school administration or contact other students’ parents to check whether the activity that has been told by the child is correct
Parents only rely on high “trust” in their children to tell all activities in school, but there are still a small number of students who do not always tell all their activities to their parents.
Currently the school has not provided enough information about student development that is easily accessible or easily known by parents
