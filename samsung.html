'STORIES' in Samsung phone Gallery app

Redesign

November 2, 2019 completed

“Stories” this is a word which take us to our own childhood where we used to listen to our grandmother or mother narrating a particular story. So here the context of story is capturing a good memory which you have passed through and making it a collection so that when you open the story you get to recollect the complete memory which had occurred in the past.

From this I started to work on where at first I gone through the interface and tried to use all the features which are related to the “stories” tag.

After playing a couple of minutes I tried to draw some features to it-
1) Why can’t we make it like a video with a music attached to it.
2) How it would be if we narrating our own story.
3) How to it would where the narrator is been seen in the video itself from a small video window in order to get the expressions of the narrator. This technique us to recollect that good movement very quickly.
4) Is it possible to add some images in between the series of images?
5) How it could be if we give some readymade templates which are having prebuilt effects, to the user so that he could make the story fast.
