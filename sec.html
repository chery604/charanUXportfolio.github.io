Homie Lock

a secure trust in your own hand.

Feb 8, 2019 Completed

About project :

The duration of the project was ten hours. The given assignment is to work on the door locking security mobile app for people to secure their home. After a comprehensive study of the whole system and considering the possible intervention points I came up with the concept of “Homie Lock”.

The Brief:

The recent advancement in smart technology design with integration of smart sensors and embedded components has rapidly improved human lives in so many areas which includes home automation, smart phones, smart healthcare, smart cars and so on. Therefore, smart home security demand serious attention and is indispensable in this era of internet of things communication over 6LowPAN model using IPv6 addresses for interconnectivities and endto end links. In this paper, the access control, embedded sensors and alarm systems are used for safeguard the device facilities in the home automation for authentication or authorization and users management over the network. However, wireless technology (such as GSM, ultrasonic sensor, and Bluetooth low-power enable) was used for controlling and monitoring home entrance, user authentication, authorization and smart devices management. An android web application was also developed and integrate with the system for home user control remotely. This developed system was equipped with a strict access control and password security features for an intruder denial or violation and also keep the neighbor’s alert of possible human attacks.

Problem

“The smart home devices are not difficult to understand; it’s the apps that are too stressful to use, too unpredictable in their behaviour, too overloaded with unnecessary functions.”

Goals:

From this I have started to design the home security system. I have studied the requirements and drawn four main features basing on the objective given in the mail. They are:
1) One time use
2) Restricted to particular time every day
3) Restricted to particular days of the week.

User Journey Mapping:

8:30 AM - Sai wakes up and gets ready for the office. He have his breakfast and gone to office. Even his father gone to office with him.
9:00 AM- Sai’s mom gone to market to get vegitables. She took one of the lock with her as his sister have to go to college at 10:00 AM, so she have to lock the door and leave.
12:00 PM – Eats lunch (sai and his father).
2:00 PM - Sai’s mom got a phone call from her friend and she asked her to come to her home to have some conversation.
4:30 PM – The conversation took so time that Sai’s mom forgot about going home as her husband and son both will get back to home.
This is how the problem arises.

Problem analysis:

1) The people in the family have diversified lives.
2) Key management (as the families will not have that many spare keys).
3) For this an app which can make their life easier and help them to manage keys, provided the security is ensured.

User case:

1) The user opens the app and selects weather he is an admin or a member in the family. If he selects as a member then he have get an OTP or have to scan the QR code from the admin app.
2) The user can sign with Gmail or Facebook or even register with the app.
3) Once the app is opened it will be having four sections:
i) Door locking.
ii) Sharing keys section.
iii) Received key section.
iv) Managing the shared keys.
4) The user can to whatever the tasks mentioned in the particular section.
i) The user access the door locking section if he wants to open or close the door. If the door is opened for 3 minutes a notification will be given to the user.
ii) The user access the sharing keys section if he wants to share a key with his known person.
iii) The user access the received key section if the user wants the key of the received user home.
iv) The user access the managing the shared key section if the wants to withdraw the key or see the details of the persons who he has shared the key.
