One Road

An employee travel booking App

December 2, 2019 completed

Abstract :

Employee Transportation a large pharmaceutical company is in the process of digitizing their employee transportation. Currently most of the planning and coordination work is handled by the facilities department and all the data is being manually tracked in spreadsheets.

The Brief:

I at first started with feel myself as an employee of a company and gathered the tasks which are to be done from the starting of going to office till returning to the home. This is how I designed the user flow for the employee.


7:30- Wake up
8:00- got ready and having tiffin
8:30- reaching the pickup stop and open the app and confirmed the pickup point in the app.Tracking the cab.
8:45 am- Caught the cab and reached office.
9:00 am- Entered to the office room.
10:00 am- Booked the office cab for a client meeting.
12:00 pm- returned from the meeting.
12:30 pm- Had lunch in the office.
5:00 pm- got out of the office.
5:15 pm- opened the app and confirmed the pickup point to return home.
6:30 pm- Returned home.

For driver user flow I imagined myself as a driver and given the user flow.

7:30 am- Wake up
8:00 am- got ready and took the cab to the office.
8:30 am- got the notification from the app for the route and started to pick the employees.
9:30 am- Reached to office by picking all the employee in the route.
10:30 am- Got a notification for the ride from the employee for a meeting.
11:00 am- dropped the employee at the destination.
11:15 am- got a notification for a ride at 1:00pm
12:00 pm- had lunch and got ready for the ride at 1:00pm
1:00 pm- picked up the employee and dropped at the destination.
3:00 pm – got the notification for a ride to drop the employee at their homes at 5:00.
5:00 pm- picked up the employees and dropped at their homes.

For supervisor user flow I at first imagined myself as the supervisor for the company.

7:30- wakeup
8:00- got ready and having tiffin
8:30- reaching the pickup stop and open the app and confirmed the pickup point in the app.Tracking the cab.
8:45 am- Caught the cab and reached office.
9:00 am- Entered to the office room.
9:30 am- got some updates in the drivers’ routes.
10:00 am- updated the routes for the drivers.
10:01 am- drivers have got the notification of the new routes.
1:00 pm- had lunch in the office.
5:00 pm- got out of the office.
5:15 pm- opened the app and confirmed the pickup point to return home.
6:30 pm- Returned home.
